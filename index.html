About Me

From Passion to Partnership

Transforming a hobby into a professional game development career

I started creating Roblox games in 2018 as a way to bring my ideas to life. What began as experimenting with simple mechanics evolved into building complex game systems that millions of players enjoy.

My breakthrough came with Hatch 1 Million Chickens, which exploded to 10.1M visits and generated $100K in its first 3 months. This success caught the attention of Playverse Studios, leading to a professional partnership that transformed my approach to game development.

At its peak, Hatch 1 Million Chickens reached #111 in Roblox earnings. According to Roblox's 2024 creator data, the top 100 creators averaged $6 million annually, while the top 1,000 averaged $820,000‚Äîup 570% since 2019. Being in that top tier validated years of learning and iteration.

I'm currently developing Grow Your Chicken, combining incremental farm mechanics with tower defense elements. This project applies everything I've learned about player retention, progression systems, and monetization strategy.

My games have been played worldwide across over 150 countries, with strong player communities in the United States, Vietnam, United Kingdom, Brazil, and beyond. Seeing players from every continent engage with my creations is incredibly rewarding and demonstrates the universal appeal of well-designed game mechanics.
